YoGita4x - The Ultimate Spiritual Card Game

Introduction

YoGita4x is a card game inspired by the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita. Players embark on a spiritual journey by following four paths - Karma Yoga, Bhakti Yoga, Jnana Yoga, and Raja Yoga - with the ultimate goal of achieving liberation (Moksha).

Game Setup

  • Each player starts with 3-4 cards in hand.
  • Place the Karma Pile face-up in the center.
  • Place the Illusion Pile face-down beside the Karma Pile.
  • Distribute starting cards to all players and prepare action cards nearby.

Gameplay Overview

Phases of a Turn

  1. Draw Phase: Draw a card from the Illusion Pile.
  2. Action Phase: Play a card, resolve effects, or overcome obstacles.
  3. Reflection Phase: Reflect and move towards spiritual progress.

Special Cards

  • Obstacle Cards: Challenges that test a player's progress.
  • Grace Cards: Help players overcome obstacles and stay aligned.
  • Yoga Path Cards: Represent progression along a chosen spiritual path.

How to Win

The first player to achieve alignment with all three paths or attain self-realization wins the game.

Advanced Modes

For experienced players, introduce alternative victory conditions and cooperative modes where players work together to overcome obstacles.
